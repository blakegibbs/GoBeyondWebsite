Mamma Mia - Here we 'Go Beyond' again ……. AGAIN!

Go Beyond’s ABBA Extravaganza returns!

12th – 19th May 2026

What's to get excited about!

After the phenomenal interest for our first ABBA themed voyage to Greece in 2025, what else could we do but bring it back for 2026!

The 2008 hit musical film, set on a Greek island to the music of Abba, was a box office smash and after listening to our guests, we included a trip to the islands where much of the film was set. We’ve never had so much interest in one holiday before, selling out quicker than any trip previously. We called it “Mama Mia - Here We Go Beyond Again’ so this year we thought, why not Go Beyond Again …….. AGAIN!

This ‘Super Trouper’ holiday will be split into three parts. To start, we’ll be up in the Greek mountains in Chania Pelion, where our hotel is based, The Manthos Mountain Resort and Spa. This luxurious hotel will give us the space we need to practise our ABBA moves because, with help from a professional dance teacher, we will actually be singing and recreating scenes from the famous film! In between rehearsals, we’ll be making the most of the great facilities on offer and just be relaxing around the hotel spa and pools in the Greek sunshine. Bliss!

The middle part of the holiday will take us by ferry to Skopelos, the island where many of the well-known scenes from the film took place. There, we will be taken on a Mamma Mia guided tour of the island and visiting locations so adored by ABBA fans. Then, we’ve an overnight stay at the Regina Hotel on the island and wake up to the glittering spectacle of the Aegean Sea. Wow!

The final two days will be spent dancing out more Mamma Mia favourites with a visit to Damouchari Beach. Here, is where one of the film’s most iconic scenes was set to the song ‘Dancing Queen’, where Donna (played by Meryl Streep), and her friends end their dance through the village streets by jumping into the water from a wooden jetty. Amazing!

So, why not join us on this ABBA extravaganza that we’re sure will bring out the ‘Dancing Queen’ in all of us!

Where you'll be staying...

At the top of a mountain, surrounded by green fir trees, overlooking the Pagasetic Gulf, lies the luxurious Manthos Resort and Spa.

We will be looked after here by the warm and friendly staff for our whole time accept one night, where we ill be staying at a hotel on the island of Skopelos.

Food, glorious food!

We will be looked after by the restaurant at the Manthos Hotel. The hotel has a fantastic menu with traditional Greek food, but please, don’t break any plates!

What you'll experience...

We will meet at a London airport and you will be supported on the flight to Thessaloniki Airport Makedonia in Greece.

Our mini-buses will take you on the 3 hour drive to the accommodation, with stops along the way.

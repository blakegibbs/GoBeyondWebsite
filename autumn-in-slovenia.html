Autumn in Slovenia – NEW FOR 2026!

Go Beyond discovers another beautiful destination

13th - 20th October 2026

What's to get excited about!

Slovenia in October is calm and mellow, accompanied by beautiful autumn colours and crisp temperatures. This mainly mountainous country, is tranquil and peaceful and will enable us to unwind and get the most out of our new voyage of discovery to this central European nation.

We will spend our time based in a gorgeous setting on the shores of Lake Bohinj, in the Julien Alps. There, we’ll witness stunning mountain sunsets over the lake, coupled with reflections of autumn colours from our accommodation, The Hotel Jezero. This elegantly, furnished hotel, hosts its own indoor pool, plus a restaurant where we can experience local as well as Mediterranean cuisine. All-in-all we believe we’ve selected the perfect hotel for us to really appreciate and take-in the beautiful scenery.

Slovenia is bordered by Italy to the west, Austria to the north, Hungary to the northeast, and Croatia to the south and southeast, and influences of all can be seen around the country. Naturally, we will be getting out and about to discover our surroundings including Slovenia’s capital, Ljuljana. There, we will be escorted on a guided tour of the city where we will learn a little of its history and take in the colourful buildings and friendly atmosphere of this increasingly popular destination.

We shall also be taking a boat trip on Lake Bohinj, enjoying a cable car up to the ski resort of Vogel where its vast views over the lake are just breathtaking. Plus, we will also take a trip to the famous fairytale scene of Lake Bled, with a boat trip visit to its picturesque island which once again is full of beauty.

To cap off this magical holiday we will visit the weird and wonderful Castle Prejama, a castle built into the mouth of a cave, extraordinary!

So, why not join us to explore this beautiful part of Europe, full of colour and wonder.

Where you'll be staying...

Hotel Jezero is centrally located by the Bohinj Lake, in the heart of the Julian Alps, where you can fill your lungs with fresh mountain air and admire superb sunsets over the mountains. Offering a wellness centre and an on-site restaurant, it also provides free Wi-Fi. The elegantly furnished rooms feature balconies with garden furniture and all modern amenities.

Food, glorious food!

All of our meals will be provided by the hotel. Enjoy delicious Slovenian cuisine in the Vrtovin restaurant with its covered summer terrace or in the à-la-carte restaurant Zlatovčica, also featuring a spacious covered terrace.

What you'll experience...

We will meet at a London airport and you will be supported on the flight to Ljubljana Airport in Slovenia. Our mini-buses will take you on the 1 hour drive to the hotel.

Ininerary

Day 1 – Travel from London to Ljubljana, travel up to our accommodation via mini bus, settle in.

Day 2 – Guided tour of Ljubljiana.

Day 3 – Visit Lake Bled and a boat trip to the island.

Day 4 – Boat trip on Lake Bohini.

Day 5 – Rest Day.

Day 6 – Castle Prejama.

Day 7 – Take cable car upto Vogel

Day 8 – Drive back to Ljubljana Airport for flight back to London.

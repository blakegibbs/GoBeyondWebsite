Welcome!

With more than fifteen years’ experience in planning, arranging and operating rewarding, exciting, fun and safe supported holidays for adults and children with ASD (autism spectrum disorder), intellectual or cognitive disabilities, or for those who just need a little extra support on their holidays, we are delighted to welcome you to Go Beyond Holidays.

Ever since we started out as Go Provence Holidays, we’ve been on an incredible journey and for our loyal guests, many of whom are firm friends now, we are continuing with our promise to seek new destinations and new adventures for us all to explore. For those new to us, we’re delighted you’re taking the time to browse through our website and we hope you get a real feel for us as a holiday provider. Initially, we always like to point out that we are renowned for our diversity, adventure and inclusion, and these values have remained very much at the heart of what we do, despite us spreading our wings and becoming more global. It’s our heritage and we’re very proud of it.

The switch in name to Go Beyond Holidays in 2020, reflected our dream to offer a much wider-reaching and exciting collection of worldwide supported holidays and travel programmes. As a result, there is more for everyone to anticipate, to enjoy and to experience with new countries being added every year to our ever-growing portfolio. There are endless possibilities out there for us to explore and, of course, more opportunities to make new friends and to create those unique, precious holiday memories which our returning guests love to share with us.

So, welcome. It’s time to holiday. It’s time to Go Beyond!

Support in the Airport

We offer support in the airport, through security and on to the flight. We support you on the flight, into the destination airport, through security and customs and collecting your luggage. We’re with you every step of the way…

Communication

We pride ourselves on excellent communication from your initial enquiry, your booking, organisation of care plans, risk assessments, payments, during the holiday and post holiday, through telephone and video calls, e-mails and WhatsApp groups.

The best food

We have our own chef, Helen, who is from Scotland and is a fabulous chef! All our meals are made from scratch, balanced and healthy, and above all are very tasty. We cater for all dietary requirements.

Great Support

We carefully select our team to reflect our philosophy and approach to supporting people. We only support our guests where needed, treat our guests as equals and value them in every way. All of our team are DBS checked.

Sustainable Travel

Go Beyond are committed to reducing our carbon footprint. Therefore, we are working with the Parc National du Verdon, near where Directors Ian and Anna live in France, who run tree planting and conservation projects.

DISCOVER ESTONIA – NEW FOR 2026!

Go Beyond explores the Baltics

21st - 29th May 2026

What's to get excited about!

You will find Estonia at the far easterly end of the Baltic Sea, to the north, Finland, and to the south, the remaining Baltic countries of Latvia and Lithuania. We invite you to join us on this nine-day journey where we will split our time equally between Estonia’s capital Tallin and further south in the heart of Soomaa National Park.

In Tallin we will be based in the 150 year old, renovated Von Stackelberg hotel. The hotel oozes style and culture overlooking Tallin’s Old Town. Here, we will be shown around the Old Town and visit Tallin’s famous TV tower providing us with panoramic views of our beautiful surroundings. We will also explore the north coast with visits to the Jagala Waterfall, Bogtrail and Altja fishing village, all of which are locations where your cameras are highly recommended!

Then it’s down to our cosy, traditional cottage in the south of Estonia. There, we will visit the seaside town of Parnu and the charming, forest town of Viljandi. The Soomaa National Park is famous for its wildlife where many animals we’re never likely to see back home roam including moose, wild boar, grey wolf, beavers, Eurasian lynx and even brown bears have been spotted.

If you have ever thought of visiting the Baltics and have never had the opportunity to, then this will be a great way to begin your Baltic exploration.

Where you'll be staying...

We will be staying in two different accommodations. In Tallin, we will be based in the 150 year old, renovated Von Stackelberg hotel. The hotel was built in 1874 and underwent complete restoration in 2015. The Hotel is located directly next to the beautiful medieval Old Town of Tallinn, only a few steps away from Toompea Castle, Nevsky Cathedral and the numerous landmarks of the best preserved medieval town in Northern Europe.

In south westernern Estonia, we will be staying in the traditional house called Soomaa Puhkeküla near Riisa in the Soomaa National Park. The wooden house features an outdoor fireplace, hot tub, and spa bath.

Food, glorious food!

In Tallin, at the Von Stackelberg hotel, they offer an ‘à la carte menu’ which is full of elegance and creative dishes. All meals will be catered here for us.

In the traditional house near Riisa, our Go Beyond Chef will be cooking for us.

What you'll experience...

We will meet at a London airport and you will be supported on the flight to Tallin Airport in Estonia. Our mini-buses will take you on the 20-minute drive to the hotel.

Ininerary

Day 1 – Travel from London to Tallin, travel up to our accommodation via mini bus, settle in.

Day 2 – Guided tour of old town Tallin

Day 3 – Visit Altja fishing village, Jagala Waterfalls and Bogtrails.

Day 4 – Use Spa in hotel and visit Tallin’s TV tower

Day 5 – Travel by mini bus to the Soomaa National Park to our second accommodation.

Day 6 – Rest Day

Day 7 – Trip to Parnu for guided tour of the town.

Day 8 – Visit the town of Viljandi, Lossipark and suspension bridge.

Day 9 - Drive back to Tallin Airport for flight back to London.
